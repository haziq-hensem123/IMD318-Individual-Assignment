The Master / The Sensei

Beyond the Belt

My fighting days gave me glory, but coaching gives me purpose. A champion is remembered for a few years; a mentor is remembered for a lifetime.

I don't just teach kids how to throw a punch. I teach them when NOT to throw it. I teach them that the discipline they learn on the mats is the same discipline they need to finish school, to respect their elders, and to build a life.

"Strong Body. Stronger Mind. Unbreakable Spirit."

The Next Generation

The youth today face battles I never had to. Distractions, peer pressure, lack of direction. In my gym, we strip all that away. We sweat together, we bleed together, and we grow together.

I am looking for the next Head Hunter. Not to take heads, but to take charge of their own destiny.
