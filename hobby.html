Hobby

The River's Peace

The ring is loud. The crowd screams, the bell rings, and the adrenaline spikes. But the river? The river demands silence. I spend hours by the water, waiting for the strike. Fishing taught me a different kind of patience—not the patience of waiting for an opening to punch, but the patience to let nature come to you. When I hold a fresh catch, I feel the same victory as lifting a belt, just… quieter.

The PKP Iron Project

During the lockdown (PKP), fights were cancelled and gyms were closed. I couldn't sit still. So, I became an engineer. I decided to build grilling machine from scratch.

Phase 1: The Skeleton
Welding the frame. I didn't have blueprints, just an idea in my head. Like a fight, you adjust your plan as you go.
Phase 2: The Mobility
I realized it was too heavy to move. So I improvised. I scavenged old tires and built a custom trolley system. Now the beast can roll anywhere.
Phase 3: The Beast
Completed. Painted. Ready for war. It’s not just an oven; it’s a tank that cooks meat.
The Feast
The best part of building it? Using it. Nothing beats the taste of Sarawakian style BBQ on a rig you built with your own hands.
